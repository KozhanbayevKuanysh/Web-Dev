Pelmeni

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
For Dough
Ingredient Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Water 3/4 cup
Egg 1
Salt 1/2 tsp
For Filling
Ingredient Quantity
Meat 400 g
Onion 1 small
Salt 1/2 tsp
Black Pepper 1/4 tsp

Steps:

1.Mix flour, water, egg, and salt. Knead dough and rest 20 minutes

2. Mix minced meat, onion, salt, and pepper.

3. Roll dough thin and cut small circles.

4. Add filling, fold, and pinch edges closed.

5. Boil in salted water 5â€“7 minutes until they float.

6. Serve hot with butter or sour cream.

Omelette

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredient Quantity
Eggs 2
Salt To taste
Butter 1 tbsp

Steps:

1. Beat the eggs with salt.

2. Melt butter in a pan.

3. Cook eggs until set.
